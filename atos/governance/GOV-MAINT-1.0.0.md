## Rules

1. **Future changes to ATOS** require the governance amendment process (GOV-FREEZE-1.0.0 + applicable Spec/Standard change management + Founder approval for constitutional layers).

2. **New work shall be built on ATOS rather than inside ATOS**, unless a formal amendment is approved.

   | Build **on** ATOS | Build **inside** ATOS |
   |---|---|
   | Product (`app/`, `lib/`, …) | Specs, Standards, constitutional text |
   | Executive operations under charters | Expanding ATOS structure without amendment |
   | Knowledge promotions via STD-002 | Ad hoc edits to frozen layers |
   | Runtime/code cutovers Founder-gated | Rewriting ATOS to fit a feature |

3. **Silent edits are prohibited.** Registries and change logs update with any approved amendment.

4. **Ask Atlas loader cutover** remains Founder-gated (GOV-COMPAT); it is not automatic under Maintenance Mode.
