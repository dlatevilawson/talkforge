## Report types

| Daily Founder Brief | Daily | Atlas | REF-R1104 |

| Incident Brief | As needed | Sentinel | STD-005 |
| Quarterly Strategy Review | Quarterly | Founder + Atlas | REF-R1109 |

## Minimum Daily Brief fields

- Date  
- Mission check (one sentence)  
- Top 3 priorities  
- Blockers / risks  
- Decisions needed from Founder  
- Evidence / sources (labeled)  
- Confidence  

## Storage

- Product-generated briefs may live in Supabase `founder_briefs` (operational).
- ATOS-governed copies or promotions use knowledge/ops registries — never silent Canonicalization.
