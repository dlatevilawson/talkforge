## Highest-leverage craft refinements (mission-aligned)

Ranked for love-of-use without new frameworks:

1. **Connecting ritual** — calm “joining Forge” state with intentional motion (reduce anxiety)  
2. **Mid-session warmth** — one non-judgmental encouragement when user finishes a turn  
3. **Momentum share moment** — optional copyable “what I’ll try next” (shareability without vanity metrics)  
4. **Return hospitality** — “Welcome back, {name}” with remembered intent  
5. **Premium empty/loading** — replace abrupt waits with cared-for pauses
