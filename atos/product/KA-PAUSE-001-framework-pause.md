## Rules (binding)

1. **Do not implement** a Human Behavior Framework (or equivalently named new stack) until it is mapped to **D-EXP / D-GROWTH / D-LEARN** (extension, no parallel OS) **or** Founder approves a new KA domain.  
2. **Do not create** parallel constitutions, competing operating systems, or unlabeled “foundations.”  
3. **Allowed:** Bugfixes; CE execution under existing gates; LP deploy if Founder separately approves; Working counsel that maps into an existing KA domain; promotion work under SPEC-003 / STD-002.  
4. **Required:** Every significant proposal states its **KA domain** (from KA-001 §4) and whether it extends an existing source or seeks a new domain.  
5. **Ask Atlas loader freeze** remains until a separate Founder Decision (GOV-COMPAT) — LOAD-MANIFEST does not auto-cut over.
