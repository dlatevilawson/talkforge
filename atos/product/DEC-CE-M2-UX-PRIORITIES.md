## Decision

### CE-M2

**Status: ✅ Accepted (Engineering)**

Stable transcript capture, order, persistence, and CE-M3 handoff shape are accepted as engineering complete.

### UX Review

**Score: 5/10**

Engineering acceptance does **not** equal product readiness. The current voice UI is too diagnostic and transcript-centered for beta.

---

## Highest-priority product work before beta

| # | Priority | Intent |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Hide developer diagnostics** from the primary interface | Connection logs, session IDs, peer states out of default view |
| 2 | **Redesign voice experience around presence** rather than transcripts | Feel with Forge; transcripts are evidence substrate, not the hero |
| 3 | **Forge behaves like a coach first**, interviewer second | Warm practice partner; interview prompts as method, not identity |
| 4 | **Simplify onboarding** — enter a session within seconds | Minimal steps from intent → voice |
| 5 | **Keep the conversation as the visual center** | Latest spoken exchange dominates the viewport |
