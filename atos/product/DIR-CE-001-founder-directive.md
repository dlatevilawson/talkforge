## Executive Context

The strategic planning phase is complete.

The Atlas Constitution is frozen.

Atlas Runtime Organization v1.0 is certified.

**FLA-001** (Forge Learning Architecture) is the canonical product specification.

**PCM-001** (Personal Communication Model) is the canonical representation of user growth.

**Product Proof Sprint 001** remains the company’s primary *proof* objective.

However, Product Proof **cannot begin** until TalkForge possesses its foundational communication capability.

Therefore, the **Communication Engine becomes the company’s immediate engineering priority.**

---

## Mission

Design, implement, and validate **CE-001 — Communication Engine v1.0**.

The Communication Engine is **not** an optional feature.

It is the **foundational interaction layer** of TalkForge.

Forge is a communication gym.

Communication must therefore become the **primary method of interaction**.

---

## Objective

Enable users to conduct **natural voice conversations** with Forge while preserving the evidence required by FLA-001.

Every completed conversation must produce:

- Natural voice interaction  
- Finalized transcript  
- Coaching evidence  
- PCM updates  
- Reality-ready session history  

---

## Engineering Principles

### 1. Voice-First

Voice is the default interaction.

Typing remains available only as a fallback.

### 2. Evidence Preservation

Realtime voice must never eliminate finalized transcripts.

Evidence is required for:

- Coaching  
- Reflection  
- PCM updates  
- Product Proof  
- Transfer measurement  

### 3. Event-First Experience

Every voice session begins with a real upcoming conversation.

Examples:

- Technical Interview  
- Behavioral Interview  
- Salary Negotiation  
- Difficult Conversation  

Forge always enters through urgency.

*(V1 engineering beachhead remains high-stakes technical interviews per RES-012; event classes above define the long-term interface pattern.)*

### 4. FLA Compliance

Every implementation decision must remain consistent with:

- Performance Lab philosophy  
- Evidence-based coaching  
- Utility before transformation  
- Reality completes the learning loop  

---

## Execution Milestones

Execute **sequentially**.

| Milestone | Success |
|---|---|
| **CE-M1** | Realtime session establishment. User presses Start → Forge speaks → conversation begins. |
| **CE-M2** | Stable finalized transcripts. Every spoken turn becomes evidence. |
| **CE-M3** | FLA coaching integration. Completed transcript automatically generates coaching. Conversation never pauses waiting for coaching. |
| **CE-M4** | Event-first voice: `/prepare` → Voice Arena → Conversation → Coaching. |
| **CE-M5** | PCM integration. Every completed session updates the Personal Communication Model. |
| **CE-M6** | Persistence and reliability. |
| **CE-M7** | Voice Product Proof — validate complete learning loop. |
| **CE-M8** | Production hardening: reconnects, latency, cost optimization, reliability. |

Detailed technical design remains in [`CE-001-communication-engine.md`](CE-001-communication-engine.md). This Directive **authorizes** that design for execution.

---

## Exit Criteria

CE-001 is complete only when:

- [ ] A user begins a voice session  
- [ ] Forge responds naturally  
- [ ] Final transcripts are preserved  
- [ ] Coaching is generated from observed evidence  
- [ ] PCM updates automatically  
- [ ] The next practice session becomes more personalized because of previous conversations

## Founder Success Definition

The milestone is complete when a first-time user can walk into TalkForge, press one button, practice a realistic high-stakes conversation entirely by voice, receive evidence-based coaching, and have that conversation permanently improve their Personal Communication Model.

At that point, TalkForge will possess its first true communication engine.

**Only then** does the company advance to Product Proof Sprint 001.
