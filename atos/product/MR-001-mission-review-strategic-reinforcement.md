## Phase 5 — Founder Report

### 1. What Should Never Change

1. **Mission:** Nobody should ever feel voiceless because they don’t know how to express themselves.  
2. **Category:** AI Communication Gym — deliberate practice, not chatbot companionship.  
3. **Vision of success:** Quality of conversations **outside** Forge.  
4. **Constitutional character:** Mission First, Human Growth, Truth, Simplicity, Practice Over Theory, User Trust, Quality, Founder Responsibility.  
5. **Forge Laws `#001`–`#010`:** Results over time; mission before features; trust; systems over hacks; decades thinking.  
6. **North Star = transfer** (RES-012 / Metrics / FLA).  
7. **FLA core doctrines:** Performance laboratory; events as interface; evidence-based coaching; reality completes the loop; utility before transformation.  
8. **Wedge is beachhead, not identity** (RES-012).  
9. **Execution sequence:** CE-001 Critical now; PPS-001 Gated until CE MVP (RES-013).  
10. **Atlas posture:** Certified, frozen, in service of TalkForge — not a peer product.

### 5. Recommendations

Each recommendation **strengthens** TalkForge’s mission by increasing the chance users become better communicators in real life — without changing Identity.

---

#### R1 — Hold the Critical path: CE + presence/trust (no interruption)

**Priority: P0**  
**Why here:** Already Founder-bound (DIR-CE-001, RES-013, DEC-CE-M2-UX). Anything else is interruption.  
**Why it strengthens mission:** Voice presence is the gym floor; without a trustworthy practice surface, deliberate practice and transfer cannot happen.  
**Action:** Continue current execution only — pre-beta UX priorities and CE milestones toward MVP. Do not open PPS, new strategy docs, or Atlas thaw.

---

#### R2 — Treat language as mission hygiene

**Priority: P0**  
**Why here:** Drift is already visible in product framing risk; costs almost no engineering; protects Identity daily.  
**Why it strengthens mission:** Keeps the company a communication gym whose *scenario* may be an interview — not an interview company that forgot the mission.  
**Action:** Prefer Conversation Type / Scenario / Mission / Coach in shipping UI and counsel. Reject “Interview Practice” as identity language.

---

#### R3 — Complete CE MVP with FLA coaching on evidence (CE-M3→)

**Priority: P1**  
**Why here:** Required to satisfy DIR-CE-001 exit intent and unlock honest PPS later.  
**Why it strengthens mission:** Transfer requires evidence-based coaching on what the user actually said — not vibes.  
**Action:** Build coaching on finalized ordered transcripts; never reintroduce diagnostic chrome as the center.

---

#### R4 — Event-first entry into practice

**Priority: P1**  
**Why here:** Specified in DIR-CE-001 and FLA; incomplete until product path forces a real upcoming conversation.  
**Why it strengthens mission:** Sessions without a real event are incomplete relative to the North Star.  
**Action:** On the Critical path, ensure voice practice is anchored to a named event before celebrating “session complete.”

---

#### R5 — Keep PPS-001 gated; run it as proof of transfer, not a launch festival

**Priority: P2** (starts only after CE MVP readiness)  
**Why here:** RES-013 gate; Metrics and PPS already define the funnel.  
**Why it strengthens mission:** Proves (or falsifies) that practice changes outside conversations — the Vision’s success definition.  
**Action:** When unlocked, instrument event → practice → reality capture; do not celebrate DAU.

---

#### R6 — Redesign mastery mechanics only under the transfer test

**Priority: P2–P3**  
**Why here:** RES-012 Decision 3 binds redesign, not removal; implementation waits until the practice loop is real.  
**Why it strengthens mission:** Progress that does not improve outside performance is false progress (Forge Law `#001`).  
**Action:** No new XP/streak pillars. Any mechanic must answer: does this improve real-world performance?

---

#### R7 — Do not ratify Forge Law `#011` until transfer is instrumented

**Priority: P3**  
**Why here:** STRAT-001 already conditioned ratification on instrumentation.  
**Why it strengthens mission:** Laws must be enforceable; an unmeasured transfer law becomes theater.  
**Action:** Wait for PPS-capable capture; then Founder may elevate `#011` as clarification of `#001`, not a new religion.

---

#### R8 — Refuse competing frameworks and “Priority OS” Canonical inflation

**Priority: P4** (explicit non-work)  
**Why here:** Founder directive for this review; Article IV Simplicity.  
**Why it strengthens mission:** Attention goes to practice quality and transfer, not meta-systems. Critical/Gated already prioritizes.  
**Action:** Classify counsel with P0–P4 when useful; do **not** ship a second operating system document unless Founder orders it.

---

#### R9 — Keep Atlas frozen; all net-new engineering on TalkForge

**Priority: P4** for Atlas expansion; **P0** for the *discipline* of refusal  
**Why here:** RES-011 / RES-012 Decision 4.  
**Why it strengthens mission:** Atlas exists to sharpen Founder judgment and protect mission — not to become the product.  
**Action:** Maintenance only until product execution reveals a true architectural need and Founder decides.
