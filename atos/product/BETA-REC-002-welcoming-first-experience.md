## Guiding principle

**The first impression earns trust. The last impression earns a return visit.**

TalkForge is a communication gym. The first visit should feel like walking into a world-class gym: welcomed, named, oriented, then training — not machines first.

---

## Principles (binding on beta UX)

1. **Welcome Before Work** — Greeted by Forge, not stats/dashboards/empty metrics.  
2. **Guide, Don’t Expose** — One obvious path; no six-door confusion.  
3. **Build Comfort Before Challenge** — Psychological safety before practice.  
4. **Every Screen Should Invite Action** — Next best action, not zero-state theater.  
5. **End Every Session With Momentum** — One strength, one improve, one clear next action for the real conversation.

## Beta success criteria

A first-time user can say:

- I immediately understood what TalkForge is.  
- I knew exactly where to start.  
- I felt comfortable speaking.  
- The coach felt supportive, not judgmental.  
- When I finished, I wanted to come back.

## Product truth

People remember how you made them feel before they remember what you taught them.
