## Purpose

Records Founder ratification of **Atlas Program Phase 1 — Executive Architecture Discovery** as the binding Executive Architecture Contract for Atlas infrastructure design and implementation.

Does not amend ATOS Specs or Standards. Does not amend Phase 0. Constrains Atlas Program work under ATOS.

---

## Resolution

The Founder hereby ratifies Atlas Program Phase 1 (Steps 1–10).

Phase 1 is the **Executive Architecture Contract**.

---

## Binding rules

1. Any Atlas infrastructure design must satisfy Phase 1.  
2. Any Atlas implementation must satisfy Phase 1 and Phase 0.  
3. If a future design conflicts with Phase 1 architecture, the design changes — not Phase 1 — unless the Founder amends it.  
4. Phase 0 remains supreme for mission/authority/responsibility; Phase 1 organizes internal architecture to satisfy Phase 0.
