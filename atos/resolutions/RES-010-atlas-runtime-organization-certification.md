## Resolution

The Founder certifies that the **Atlas Runtime Organization** has successfully completed the organizational validation program (**WP-S0 through WP-S5**).

The organization is **certified for continued development**.

### Certification applies to

- Runtime organizational model (`AIO-*` staff)  
- Delegation framework (Core task assignment + staff facades)  
- Governance boundaries (including GUARD ≠ Sentinel)  
- Validation architecture (ATLAS-P6-EXEC / ATLAS-ORG-VAL evidence)

### Certification does **not** authorize

- Automatic promotion of experimental capabilities  
- Bypass of governance controls  
- Enabling Founder-visible functionality (`ATLAS_RUNTIME_FOUNDER_VISIBLE`) without separate approval  
- Loader freeze lift without separate Founder Decision  

### Standing rule

All future development shall proceed within the validated organizational framework unless superseded by a formally approved architectural revision.
