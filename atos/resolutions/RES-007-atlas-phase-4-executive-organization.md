## Purpose

Records Founder ratification of **Atlas Program Phase 4 — Executive Organization** as the binding Executive Organization Contract for TalkForge.

Does not amend ATOS Specs or Standards. Does not amend Phases 0–3. Constrains Atlas Program and executive organization work under ATOS.

---

## Resolution

The Founder hereby ratifies Atlas Program Phase 4 (Steps 1–6).

Phase 4 is the **Executive Organization Contract**.

---

## Binding rules

1. Atlas coordinates the executive organization but does not constitute the organization.  
2. Each executive office owns a defined domain, possesses explicit authority boundaries, and remains accountable for its responsibilities.  
3. Every responsibility has exactly one owner.  
4. Future organizational growth shall extend this contract rather than bypass it.  
5. If implementation or future design conflicts with Phase 4, the implementation or design changes — not the contract — unless the Founder amends it.  
6. Phases 0–3 remain binding for Atlas mission, architecture, infrastructure, and runtime.
