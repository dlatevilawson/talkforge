## Resolution

1. **KA-001 — Unified Knowledge Architecture** is **Approved** and becomes **Authoritative**.  
2. **Purpose (binding):** The Knowledge Architecture exists so TalkForge becomes progressively wiser in service of the people it exists to help — not to organize information for its own sake.  
3. **Stewardship of truth (binding):**  
   - No person, AI agent, or founder owns the truth.  
   - They are temporary stewards of humanity’s current best understanding.  
   - Canonical knowledge is preserved because it is the strongest evidence-supported, mission-aligned explanation — not because it is old.  
   - Better evidence is not a threat; it is how the institution fulfills its purpose.  
4. Atlas is **authorized to reorganize** existing documents into KA domain homes per KA-001 §10 / KA-REORG-001 — without amending ATOS Specs and without unilaterally lifting the Ask Atlas loader freeze (GOV-COMPAT).  
5. After reorganization is recorded complete, **KA-PAUSE-001** lifts for new frameworks **only** when they map to an existing KA domain or Founder approves a new domain.  
6. Accepted doctrine (FLA, PCM, AMD, ELM, PCI, CE gates) remains in force and is placed under its proper domain index.
