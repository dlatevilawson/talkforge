## Resolution

1. **STRAT-001** is **Accepted with Amendments**. Strategic planning phase is complete.  
2. The following Founder Decisions are binding:

### Decision 1 — Wedge ICP & Spear Mission (Approved, Refined)

**V1 beachhead:** High-agency technical professionals preparing for **high-stakes technical interviews**.

This is the V1 wedge — **not** the long-term market.

**Long-term mission unchanged:** Help people master life’s most important conversations.

### Decision 2 — North Star Metric (Approved)

North Star is **transfer**, not engagement.

Success = improved performance in **real-world conversations**, not time spent inside Forge.

Every feature must increase the probability that users perform better **outside** the product.

### Decision 3 — Gamification (Redesign, Not Remove)

Gamification is **not eliminated**.

It is **redesigned**: reinforce deliberate practice, visible progress, and mastery — not dopamine loops.

Every game mechanic must answer: **Does this improve real-world performance?** If not, it does not belong in Forge.

### Decision 4 — Engineering Focus (Approved)

All **net-new engineering capacity** is directed toward TalkForge.

**Atlas Organization v1.0 is constitutionally frozen.**

Future Atlas work occurs only when product execution reveals an architectural need.

Atlas is **infrastructure**, not the primary project.

3. **Product doctrines** in Founder Strategic Refinements are ratified and embodied in **FLA-001** (Forge Learning Architecture v1.0):

   - Forge is a Performance Laboratory  
   - Events Are the Interface  
   - Coaching Must Be Evidence-Based  
   - Reality Completes the Learning Loop  
   - Utility Before Transformation  

4. **FLA-001** is Institutional Approved as **Product Canonical** — governing specification for coaching agents, simulations, and product design (does not amend ATOS constitutional Specs).  

5. **PPS-001 — Product Proof Sprint 001** is the active company objective:

   > Demonstrate that Forge measurably improves a user’s preparation for a real, high-stakes conversation and use real-world outcomes to strengthen future coaching.

6. Architecture is no longer the bottleneck. **Execution is.**
