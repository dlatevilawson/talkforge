## Resolution

1. **PCI-001 — Product Craftsmanship Initiative** is **Accepted** as Authoritative Founder Update.  
2. **Stage shift:** Product objective is to **build a product people immediately love using** — engineering and craftsmanship with equal discipline.  
3. **Mandatory:** Every Atlas product review includes a **Craftsmanship Review** per PCI-001 §11. Experiences that merely function well are not sufficient for approval counsel.  
4. **Four-dimension standard** (Functional / Emotional / Cognitive / Craftsmanship) governs feature evaluation.  
5. **Share test** applies: would someone excitedly send this to a friend after five minutes?  
6. **Mission protection:** Craftsmanship amplifies the communication-gym mission; it does not replace Identity (MR-001).  
7. **Non-amendments:**  
   - Atlas Runtime Organization remains stable / frozen (RES-011 / RES-012). No org redesign.  
   - No ATOS constitutional Spec amendment.  
   - “Design Organization” in PCI-001 is a **reasoning lens**, not a new executive org.  
8. **PPS-001** remains **Gated** (RES-013).  
9. **BETA-REC-002** remains the welcoming-first execution baseline; PCI-001 raises the craftsmanship bar on top of it.
