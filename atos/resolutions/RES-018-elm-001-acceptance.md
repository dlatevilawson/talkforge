## Resolution

1. **ELM-001 — Experience Layer Model** is **Accepted** as the **foundational language for every department**.  
2. The five layers — **Human · Moment · Emotion · Decision · Transformation** — are binding intake for significant product, design, engineering, coaching, marketing, research, and strategy work.  
3. ELM-001 **operationalizes** FLA events, AMD-001 dignity/emotion, and PCI craft — it does **not** create a competing Identity or replace FLA-001.  
4. Atlas product reviews shall require ELM-001 answers **plus** the Human Dignity Test **plus** Craftsmanship Review before recommending approval.  
5. Transformation success = measurable emotional shift (Arrival→Departure), not perfection; transfer readiness remains North Star companion.  
6. **Non-amendments:** Atlas org unchanged. Mission unchanged. PPS-001 remains Gated.
