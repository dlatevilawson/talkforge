## Resolution

1. **TEA-001 — TalkForge Enterprise Architecture** is **Accepted** and becomes **Authoritative** as the institutional **communication architecture** (not a parallel framework or constitution).  
2. **KA-001** remains the map of **where** knowledge belongs; **TEA-001** is how domains **function together** as one living institution.  
3. **Foundational principle (binding):** Every domain is consumer, producer, and steward of institutional knowledge. No framework may be an informational dead end.  
4. **Six permanent enterprise domains:** Identity · Governance · Knowledge · Operations · Product · Learning — with the institutional knowledge flow in TEA-001 §3.  
5. **TEA-MAP-001** is the binding coherence audit for existing frameworks; gaps are remediations, not license for new parallel foundations.  
6. **Future frameworks** require TEA §10 declarations (domain ownership, I/O, dependencies, governance, knowledge flow) **and** KA domain mapping (KA-PAUSE-001). No framework outside TEA unless Founder establishes a new enterprise domain.  
7. **Human Behavior Framework** remains unimplemented until it passes TEA §10 and KA-PAUSE rules; its *target* enterprise home is **E-KNOW**.  
8. Ask Atlas loader freeze and ATOS Specs remain unchanged by this Resolution.
