## Resolution

1. **DIR-CE-001** is ratified as binding Founder Directive.  
2. **CE-001 Communication Engine v1.0** is **Approved for Execution** at **Critical** priority — the company’s **immediate engineering priority**.  
3. CE-001 is the **foundational interaction layer** of TalkForge, not an optional feature. Voice is the default interaction; typing is fallback only.  
4. **FLA-001** remains the canonical product specification.  
5. **PCM-001** is Institutional Approved as the **canonical representation of user growth** (Product Canonical).  
6. **Product Proof Sprint 001 (PPS-001) may not begin** until CE-001 reaches **MVP readiness** (DIR-CE-001 Dependency). Voice is a prerequisite for Product Proof.  
7. Prior PPS scaffolding (e.g. event-first prepare, text coach, reality capture) remains available as infrastructure but does **not** constitute the start of Product Proof under this Resolution.  
8. Atlas Constitution remains frozen. Atlas Runtime Organization v1.0 remains certified and frozen as infrastructure. No governance redesign is authorized by this Resolution.
