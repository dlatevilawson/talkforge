## Purpose

Records Founder ratification of **Atlas Program Phase 5 — Atlas Internal Organization** as the binding **Atlas Internal Organization Contract**.

Does not amend ATOS Specs or Standards. Does not amend Phases 0–4. Does not alter company executive ownership (ATLAS-P4).

---

## Resolution

The Founder hereby ratifies Atlas Program Phase 5, including the required correction:

**Explicit boundary specification between AIO-GUARD and Sentinel (EXEC-SENTINEL)** — making it impossible for future implementations to interpret GUARD as a replacement for, or duplicate of, Sentinel.

Phase 5 is the **Atlas Internal Organization Contract**.

---

## Binding rules

1. Atlas internal offices (`AIO-*`) staff Atlas; they are not company `EXEC-*` offices.  
2. AIO-GUARD validates Atlas emission integrity only; Sentinel owns company engineering integrity truth.  
3. GUARD must never replace, duplicate, override, absorb, or speak as Sentinel.  
4. One responsibility → one owner remains absolute across AIO and EXEC planes.  
5. Future Atlas staff growth extends this contract rather than bypasses it.  
6. If implementation conflicts with Phase 5, the implementation changes — not the contract.
