## Resolution

1. **AMD-001 — Human Dignity Standard** is **Accepted** as a **derived constitutional principle**.  
2. Atlas constitutional review finds **no contradiction** with Mission, Constitution (esp. Articles II, VI, Final Principle), Founder Brief, Forge Laws, or FLA-001. It **strengthens** them.  
3. **Brand Principle #001 (Start with Humanity)** and **Respect Standard #002 (Feedback increases self-belief before performance)** are binding on all TalkForge workstreams.  
4. **Human Dignity Test** is a **mandatory approval gate** for product, engineering, coaching, marketing, and design reviews — alongside PCI-001 Craftsmanship Review.  
5. **Forge Law #011** is adopted: *Protect the person’s dignity before pursuing the product’s objectives.*  
6. Constitution is amended by addition of **Article IX — Human Dignity** (see `atlas/constitution.md`), per Founder proposal and this Resolution.  
7. Atlas shall maintain an audit of user touchpoints against this standard (`HDS-AUDIT-001`) and prioritize corrective actions by user impact.  
8. **Non-amendments:** Atlas Runtime Organization unchanged. Mission statement unchanged. MR-001 posture unchanged (reinforce, do not replace Identity).
