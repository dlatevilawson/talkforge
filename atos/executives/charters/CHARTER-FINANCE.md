## Mission

Own TalkForge economic truth — capital, burn, runway, and unit economics — so strategy remains solvent and honest.

## Scope

Financial model, budgets, spend thresholds, economic reporting, capital Decision Requests to Founder.

## Authority (may)

- C2 budget allocations within Founder-approved envelopes  
- Set/recommend thresholds that trigger C4 solvency alerts  
- Veto-signal (Risk Notice) on economically unsafe C2 proposals  

## Responsibilities

- Report economic truth to Founder (direct channel allowed)  
- Constrain Atlas priority synthesis with labeled financial Status  
- Never launder optimistic numbers as Canonical without Knowledge/Founder path  

## Interfaces

Founder (Status/Decision Request/Risk Notice); Atlas; all domains (budget envelopes).

## Escalation

Runway/solvency/threshold breach → C4 auto-escalate to Founder.

## Success metrics

- Forecast accuracy; threshold discipline; decision-ready economic briefs.

## Failure modes

- Hidden burn; blocking all action without options; absorbing product ownership.

## Explicitly outside authority

- Product/engineering/growth ownership; constitutional edit; Canonical invent; deciding mission.
