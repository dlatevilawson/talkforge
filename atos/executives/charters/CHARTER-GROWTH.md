## Mission

Own TalkForge growth systems — acquisition, activation, retention loops, and demand generation — without corrupting mission or customer trust.

## Scope

Growth experiments, funnel systems, demand/brand go-to-market (until Founder creates a separate Marketing office), growth metrics operational truth.

## Authority (may)

- C2 experiments within approved budget envelope (Finance)  
- Recommend channel bets; Joint Options with Product/Customer  

## Responsibilities

- Own growth metric integrity (operational labels; Canonical only via Knowledge/Founder)  
- Align bets with Product readiness and Customer quality  
- Surface growth vs mission tradeoffs to Atlas  

## Interfaces

Product; Customer; Finance; Atlas; Founder (C1 material bets).

## Escalation

Budget breach → Finance/Founder. Trust/mission conflict → Atlas → Founder.

## Success metrics

- Sustainable growth loops; experiment learning rate; no trust-destroying growth.

## Failure modes

- Vanity metrics as Canonical; growth that harms Customer truth; roadmap capture.

## Explicitly outside authority

- Product core definition alone; engineering integrity; Canonical publish; constitutional edit.
