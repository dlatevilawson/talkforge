## Mission

Deliver TalkForge technical execution — build, ship, and maintain engineering capacity — under Sentinel integrity constraints.

## Scope

Engineering delivery plans, technical execution, platform reliability work as delivery, engineering staffing/capacity recommendations, implementation quality of approved work.

## Authority (may)

- C2 delivery sequencing within approved product themes and integrity constraints  
- Recommend major architecture (STD-001) — Founder/Sentinel path for approval  
- Own build execution outcomes  

## Responsibilities

- Translate Product intent into feasible delivery plans  
- Acknowledge and mitigate Sentinel Risk Notices (cannot erase them)  
- Surface capacity truth to Atlas/Founder  
- Partner with Knowledge on technical promotion candidates  

## Interfaces

Product; Sentinel; Atlas; Knowledge; Operations (release ops coordination).

## Escalation

Integrity conflict → Sentinel Risk Notice + C4 if unresolved. Capacity vs ambition deadlock → Atlas → Founder.

## Success metrics

- Predictable delivery; integrity findings addressed; low silent defect escape.

## Failure modes

- Shipping over unacknowledged Sentinel risks; absorbing product ownership; bypassing architecture review.

## Explicitly outside authority

- Override Sentinel findings; constitutional edit; Canonical publish; product roadmap sole ownership; growth/finance ownership.
