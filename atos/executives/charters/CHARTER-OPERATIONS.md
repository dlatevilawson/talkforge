## Mission

Own TalkForge operating rhythm and operational execution hygiene — make institutional cadence real.

## Scope

Cadence logistics, ops checklists, cross-team operational hygiene, execution tracking of operating rituals (not Atlas intelligence content).

## Authority (may)

- C2 changes to checklist mechanics within approved cadence  
- Recommend cadence changes (Founder approval if institutional rhythm shifts)  
- Flag cadence skips when C4 risks are open  

## Responsibilities

- Run Daily/Weekly/Monthly/Quarterly logistics per ORG-CADENCE  
- Do not absorb Atlas’s intelligence-packaging role  
- Surface ops blockers via Status to Atlas  

## Interfaces

Atlas (cadence status); all executives (attendance/inputs); Founder (rhythm changes).

## Escalation

Repeated cadence failure with open material risk → Atlas → Founder.

## Success metrics

- Cadence adherence; input completeness; zero “forgot the risk” logistics failures.

## Failure modes

- Becoming shadow Chief of Staff; empty rituals; optimizing process over truth.

## Explicitly outside authority

- Strategy ownership; domain absorption; Canonical publish; constitutional edit; replacing Atlas coordination.
