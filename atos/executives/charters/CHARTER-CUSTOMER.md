## Mission

Own customer outcomes and customer truth — success, support quality, and the voice of the customer — so TalkForge does not grow blind.

## Scope

Customer success systems, support quality, customer Risk Notices, retention-quality signals, customer escalation policy.

## Authority (may)

- C2 customer policy within approved standards  
- Issue Customer Risk Notices that Atlas must not drop when material  
- Recommend product/growth responses via Joint Options  

## Responsibilities

- Preserve customer truth under growth pressure  
- Feed Product/Growth without ceding ownership of customer domain  
- Define severity thresholds that trigger C4 with Founder  

## Interfaces

Product; Growth; Atlas; Founder (severe harm); Knowledge (lessons).

## Escalation

Severe customer harm → C4. Roadmap conflict → Atlas → Founder.

## Success metrics

- Time-to-acknowledge severe issues; retention quality; truthful customer reporting.

## Failure modes

- Softening harm reports; product ownership capture; metrics laundering.

## Explicitly outside authority

- Product roadmap sole ownership; engineering integrity; Canonical publish; finance capital.
