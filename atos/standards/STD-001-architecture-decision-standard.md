ATOS Standard 001 — Architecture Decision Standard (ADS)

Version: 1.0 (Draft)

Purpose

The Architecture Decision Standard establishes the required process for making significant architectural, organizational, and strategic decisions within TalkForge.

Its purpose is to ensure decisions are reasoned, evidence-based, documented, and aligned with the long-term mission of the organization.

⸻

Scope

Applies to:

* Architecture
* Product strategy
* Organizational design
* AI systems
* Technology selection
* Governance changes
* Executive recommendations

⸻

Decision Lifecycle

Every significant decision shall follow this sequence:

1. Problem Definition
2. Context Collection
3. Evidence Collection
4. Assumption Identification
5. Alternative Generation
6. Tradeoff Analysis
7. Risk Assessment
8. Long-Term Impact Analysis
9. Recommendation
10. Executive Review
11. Founder Approval (when required)
12. Decision Record

⸻

Required Outputs

Every Architecture Decision Record (ADR) shall include:

* Decision ID
* Date
* Author
* Problem
* Context
* Evidence
* Alternatives
* Tradeoffs
* Risks
* Final Recommendation
* Decision
* Review History

⸻

Principles

* Facts before opinions.
* Evidence before conclusions.
* Multiple alternatives before commitment.
* Long-term thinking over short-term optimization.
* Every decision remains traceable.

⸻

Success Criteria

* Decisions are documented.
* Reasoning is reproducible.
* Alternatives are evaluated.
* Organizational learning improves.
