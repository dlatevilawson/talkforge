ATOS Standard 002 — Knowledge Promotion Standard (KPS)

Version: 1.0 (Draft)

Purpose

Defines the governed process by which information becomes institutional knowledge.

⸻

Promotion Pipeline

1. Observation
2. Investigation
3. Evidence Collection
4. Evidence Review
5. Recommendation
6. Executive Review
7. Institutional Approval
8. Canonical Publication
9. Historical Preservation

⸻

Evidence Requirements

Every promotion requires:

* Source
* Evidence
* Confidence Level
* Reviewer
* Date
* Version

⸻

Confidence Levels

* Observation
* Hypothesis
* Investigated
* Verified
* Canonical
* Superseded
* Archived

⸻

Promotion Rules

Knowledge cannot skip stages.

No executive may independently create institutional knowledge.

Institutional approval is mandatory.

⸻

Canonical Publication

Approved knowledge becomes:

* Searchable
* Versioned
* Traceable
* Governed

⸻

Success Criteria

* One canonical truth.
* Evidence supports authority.
* Historical lineage preserved.

⸻
