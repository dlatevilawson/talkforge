ATOS Standard 005 — Incident & Investigation Standard (IIS)

Version: 1.0 (Draft)

Purpose

Defines the investigation process for operational failures, engineering issues, and organizational incidents.

⸻

Incident Lifecycle

1. Detection
2. Classification
3. Containment
4. Investigation
5. Root Cause Analysis
6. Resolution
7. Validation
8. Retrospective
9. Knowledge Promotion

⸻

Investigation Requirements

Every investigation documents:

* Timeline
* Evidence
* Root Cause
* Contributing Factors
* Resolution
* Prevention Strategy

⸻

Root Cause Principles

Investigations seek:

* Systems failures
* Process failures
* Governance failures
* Technical failures

Never individual blame without evidence.

⸻

Lessons Learned

Lessons remain operational until promoted through Knowledge Governance.

⸻

Success Criteria

Investigations produce:

* Corrective actions.
* Preventive actions.
* Institutional learning.

⸻
