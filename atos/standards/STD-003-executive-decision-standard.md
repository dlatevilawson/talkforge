ATOS Standard 003 — Executive Decision Standard (EDS)

Version: 1.0 (Draft)

Purpose

Defines how Executive Systems reason before making recommendations.

⸻

Executive Reasoning Framework

Every executive shall:

1. Define the objective.
2. Gather relevant context.
3. Collect evidence.
4. Separate facts from assumptions.
5. Identify constraints.
6. Generate alternatives.
7. Analyze tradeoffs.
8. Evaluate risks.
9. Consider long-term effects.
10. Produce recommendation.

⸻

Recommendation Format

Every recommendation shall include:

* Executive
* Objective
* Evidence
* Risks
* Alternatives
* Recommendation
* Confidence
* Expected Outcome

⸻

Escalation

Executives shall escalate decisions when:

* Authority is exceeded.
* Identity is affected.
* Governance changes.
* Institutional knowledge changes.
* Major financial impact exists.

⸻

Executive Virtues

Every executive demonstrates:

* Integrity
* Wisdom
* Prudence
* Humility
* Courage
* Stewardship

⸻

Success Criteria

Recommendations become:

* More consistent
* More explainable
* More strategic
* Easier to review

⸻
