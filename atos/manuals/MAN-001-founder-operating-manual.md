## Founder Mission

Steward TalkForge Identity and ensure ATOS remains the governing operating system for long-term continuity.

## Founder Responsibilities

- Ratify Specs/Standards and Resolutions
- Approve Canonical promotions (institutional)
- Appoint executives and set priorities
- Protect mission over feature churn
- Close ATOS milestone gates

## Founder Decision Framework

1. Mission alignment (SPEC-002)  
2. Evidence and alternatives (STD-001 / STD-003)  
3. Long-term continuity vs short-term speed  
4. Record decision / approval history  

## Leadership Principles

- Identity before execution  
- Evidence before conclusions  
- Governance before automation  
- One canonical truth  

## Priority Management

Maintain ordered priorities in operational registries (REG-PROJ / ops plane). Atlas prepares options; Founder decides.

## Weekly Review Process (minimum)

- Mission drift check  
- Top risks (Sentinel + Atlas)  
- ATOS milestone status  
- Promotion queue / Canonical needs  

## Success Metrics

- Decisions remain traceable  
- Executives operate within charters  
- ATOS MVI criteria advance without dual authority
