# TALKFORGE — FOUNDER BRIEF v1.0

## Company

Name: TalkForge

Category: AI Communication Gym

---

## Mission

Nobody should ever feel voiceless because they don't know how to express themselves.

TalkForge helps people become more confident communicators through deliberate practice with an AI coach.

We are not building another chatbot.

We are building the world's best place to practice communication.

---

## Vision

Communication changes lives.

The better someone communicates, the more opportunities they create, the more people they influence, and the stronger their relationships become.

Success is measured by conversations outside the app.

---

## Product Philosophy

TalkForge is a communication gym.

Users improve through deliberate practice, immediate feedback, reflection, and repetition.

Every interaction should make the user a better communicator.

---

## Core Principles

- Mission before features.
- Build trust through honesty.
- Practice beats theory.
- Progress is measured by results, not time.
- Nobody should ever feel voiceless.
